Enjoy Curitiba!

One of the most amazing capital cities in Brazil and of the best cities to live in the world. It is the biggest city in the south of the country with over 2 million inhabitants. Curitiba was elected the most intelligent city of the country, and it is a referal in living quality. It's a city with great european influency that has contributed to a beautiful cultural and gastronomical legacy. One of the great characteristics of the city is the efficiency on public transportation and urban mobility, and a great artistic production.
